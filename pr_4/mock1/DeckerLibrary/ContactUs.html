The Decker Library is located at 1401 W. Mount Royal Ave. Baltimore, MD 21217. It is the campus Library of the Maryland Institute College of Art (MICA), located on the first floor and lower level of the Bunting Center. The Decker Library Catalog is available for searching the collection from any location, on- or off-campus. From off-campus you will be prompted to log in with your MICA user name and password (same as the My MICA Portal log-in). If you are logged in to the Library Catalog, you can then use Off-Campus links to research databases without a second log-in.
The public is welcome to use Decker Library for art-related research. Borrowing priveleges do not extend to members of the public.
