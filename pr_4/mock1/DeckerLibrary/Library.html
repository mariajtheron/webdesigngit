The Decker Library is the campus Library of the Maryland Institute College of Art, located on the first floor and lower level of the Bunting Center. The library provides a wide range of services and facilities for research and instruction, it contains almost 64,000 monographs and 325 current periodical subscriptions, and provides online access to more than 5,500 full-text journal titles, and hundreds of digital reference sources in art and numerous other subjects.

Computer workstations are available on the Library, both iMacs and PCs. The entire Library is wireless enabled. Laptop use is supported on the lower level of the library, with pull-down electrical outlets above the study tables and a Laptop Lounge with flexible seating.
